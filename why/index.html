Why This Site Exists

Back to index · Methodology

Federal agencies publish documents for public comment through Regulations.gov. Quality comments are especially beneficial, but the platform does not make it easy to quickly see what matters most.

Relevant issues can be hard to find because metadata is sparse and default ordering is not useful for triage. Regulations Observer presents NPRMs and other public-comment material with tools to help average citizens find issues they care about and make informed comments.
