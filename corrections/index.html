If you find an error, email regs@kiteframe.app with the docket/document ID and what appears wrong.

  • Factual/linking errors: corrected as soon as practical, usually same day.
  • Scoring or classification disputes: reviewed and either corrected or documented with rationale.
  • Prompt/summary quality issues: revised in the next summary compile pass.

Substantive corrections are reflected in source data and site export, not hidden in silent edits.
