## Step 1 — Claim Stress Test

### Claim: RACT determination is adequate

* **Underlying assumption:**
  State comparison to CTG/NSPS ≈ sufficient demonstration of RACT

* **Failure scenario:**
  If facility-specific conditions differ (e.g., operational variability, leak frequency), generalized comparisons may understate emissions

* **Procedural implication:**
  Record may lack **facility-specific engineering justification**

---

### Claim: Emissions below thresholds imply adequacy

* **Underlying assumption:**
  Thresholds (e.g., 6 tpy) are appropriate proxies for RACT applicability

* **Failure scenario:**
  Small absolute emissions but **high marginal reduction potential** could exist

* **Procedural implication:**
  Threshold reliance without marginal analysis → **potential under-control**

---

### Claim: No rubber tire manufacturing sources exist

* **Underlying assumption:**
  NAICS/SIC + staff knowledge + web search = complete inventory

* **Failure scenario:**

  * Misclassified facilities
  * Small or intermittent operations
  * Emerging or repurposed facilities

* **Procedural implication:**
  Negative declaration vulnerable to **classification blind spots**
