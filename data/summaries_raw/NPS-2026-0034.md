# Step 2 — Essence of the Rule

## Observed Facts

* The rule proposes amendments to **36 CFR Part 13** governing Alaska park units. 
* It revises the definition of **“Trapping”** and removes **“Furbearer.”** 
* It deletes regulatory provisions from earlier rules (2015, 2017, 2024). 
* It allows hunting and trapping in national preserves subject to federal and non-conflicting state law. 

## Inference

The rule primarily **rolls back federal restrictions introduced in earlier NPS regulations**, restoring greater alignment with Alaska state wildlife regulations.
