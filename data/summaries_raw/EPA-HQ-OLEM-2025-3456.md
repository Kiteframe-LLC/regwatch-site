## Step 1 — Claim Stress Test

### Claim: 24-month timeline is sufficient

* **Underlying assumption:** Industry can transition from <1% adoption to 100% in 24 months
* **Failure scenario:**

  * Small/rural generators lack IT infrastructure
  * Training lag persists
* **Procedural implication:** Arbitrary timeline → APA vulnerability

---

### Claim: Mandate “breaks the status quo”

* **Underlying assumption:** Lack of adoption is behavioral, not structural
* **Failure scenario:** Adoption barriers are structural (cost, connectivity)
* **Procedural implication:** Misdiagnosed problem → inadequate reasoning

---

### Claim: Administrative burden decreases

* **Underlying assumption:** Electronic systems universally reduce burden
* **Failure scenario:**

  * Increased compliance burden for low-volume entities
  * onboarding + IT costs exceed savings
* **Procedural implication:** Failure to consider heterogeneous impacts

---

### Claim: Registry unnecessary

* **Underlying assumption:** All manifests flow through e-Manifest system
* **Failure scenario:**

  * Edge cases (offline, emergency, cross-border)
* **Procedural implication:** Loss of fallback mechanism
