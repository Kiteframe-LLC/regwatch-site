# Step 1 — Claim Stress Test

### Claim

Banks must obtain **FDIC approval** before issuing payment stablecoins through subsidiaries.

**Underlying assumption**

Stablecoin issuance presents **banking-system risk** requiring supervisory review.

**Failure scenario**

If stablecoin activities are conducted through:

* offshore affiliates
* joint ventures
* fintech partnerships

they may fall outside the defined “subsidiary issuance” framework.

**Procedural implication**

The rule could unintentionally create **regulatory arbitrage**.

---

### Claim

The GENIUS Act authorizes this supervisory approval structure.

**Underlying assumption**

The statute clearly delegates **approval authority to FDIC**.

**Failure scenario**

If statutory language only authorizes regulation of **insured institutions themselves**, approval of subsidiary activity might exceed statutory scope.

**Procedural implication**

Possible **APA vulnerability** if interpretation is not thoroughly explained.
