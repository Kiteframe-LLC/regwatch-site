## Step 1 — Claim Stress Test

### Claim: Compensation differential creates fraud risk

* **Underlying assumption:** Providers respond strongly to rate differences
* **Failure scenario:** Providers already have safeguards; misclassification rare
* **Procedural implication:** Rule may overcorrect without evidence → arbitrary

---

### Claim: Automated IP Relay ≈ ASR-only IP CTS

* **Underlying assumption:** Functional equivalence at technical and user levels
* **Failure scenario:** Text-to-speech component materially changes service value
* **Procedural implication:** Misclassification could distort compensation policy

---

### Claim: Numbering directory no longer needed

* **Underlying assumption:** VoIP routing substitutes fully for directory
* **Failure scenario:** Edge cases (inter-provider calls, emergency routing)
* **Procedural implication:** Risk of degraded access → functional equivalence violation

---

### Claim: Compliance reports unnecessary

* **Underlying assumption:** On-demand reporting is equivalent to routine reporting
* **Failure scenario:** Reduced visibility → delayed fraud detection
* **Procedural implication:** Weakens administrative record for enforcement
