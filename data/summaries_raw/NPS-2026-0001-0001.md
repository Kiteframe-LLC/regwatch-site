# Step 18 — Decision Brief

### Leverage Point 1

**Key sentence**

> “The NPS does not anticipate there will be conflicts between vehicles, equestrian users, hikers, and bicyclists.” 

### Routine description

The rule authorizes expanded mixed-use recreation on park trails and roads. Mixed-use trails often produce **user-conflict dynamics**, especially where bicycle speeds differ significantly from pedestrian or equestrian travel.

### Regulatory implications

Without clear conflict-mitigation strategies (speed limits, signage, trail separation), this assumption could weaken the agency’s reasoning under NEPA.

### Real-world consequence

User conflict can:

* reduce safety
* degrade visitor experience
* create pressure to restrict use after implementation

### Worth a comment?

**Moderate value**

It would likely prompt the agency to document mitigation strategies in the final rule or administrative record.
